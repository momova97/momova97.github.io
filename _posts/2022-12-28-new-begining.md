## My hopes and dreams for the new year

The year 2022 was a good year for me. I started my life in Canada almost a year ago; at that time, I had no friends, no job no clear vision of where I was going. I didn't even have a permanent place to live.
Now I have all of them. I thank god for my life; it wasn't easy, but I did it. I managed to survive against all the odds. Now I have friends, family, and everything I ever wanted.

Now let's cycle back to this website. I bought the domain for this site almost a year ago. I changed the entire design twice until I chose to go with the simplest version, which is this current version. I learned a lot about web design and all tech around it.

Next year I'm going to use this site more often. I will write more and write as frequently as daily. I want to practice writing as it's one of the areas I need to pay more attention to. But the question remains what I will write about?

This is a personal blog, and It can be about whatever I want, but what do I want to write about? I don't know the answer yet, but I will find it soon.
